COVID-19 Data Monitoring

2020-08-11

I recently began a project utilizing the incidence of COVID-19 in relation to other variables. In order to ensure my data is updated frequently enough, I wanted to have a method of monitoring COVID-19, real time, to see when any major changes occur. In order to achieve this, I built an interactive shiny app to monitor the data.

To build my shiny app, I am defining inputs as follows: * country: the name of the country * type: the type of case (i.e. confirmed, tested, recovered) * level: granularity of the occurance (i.e. country, region, state) * date: range of data



I now have a simple shiny application with interactive abilities so I can view data of interest to my project and update the data in my project as needed. For clear examination of the data, I can choose specific locations of interest, alter the granularity of the location(s), and choose the type of COVID-19 cases I am interested in, all over a specifiable date range.
